 MB86115A - 2G LSI - "MUSE Sound Processor" (Sony SAU-500MN, Sony MSC-4000)
 CXD1175AM - 8-bit 20 MSPS Video A/D Converter (Sony SAU-500MN, ?Sony SAU-300MN?)
 CXD2020Q/AQ - MUSE-NTSC Digital Chroma Decoder / MUSE Processor (Sony SAU-500MN, ?Sony SAU-300MN?)
 CXD2021Q - MUSE-NTSC Signal Conversion Decoder / NTSC Processor (Sony SAU-500MN, ?Sony SAU-300MN?)
 CXK1206AM - Frame memory SRAM (Sony SAU-500MN - 3x, ?Sony SAU-300MN - 3x?)
 CXP5068H-230Q - ?4-bit? MCU (Sony SAU-500MN)
 PD00601 - Digital Filter (Audio use) (Sony SAU-500MN 2x)
